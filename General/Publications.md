# Citation

If you have used Vital Recorder in your research and would like to describe the program and the data obtained in the Methods section, cite the following paper to reduce the inconvenience of describing in detail how to obtain the data.

- Lee HC, Jung CW. Vital Recorder-a free research tool for automatic recording of high-resolution time-synchronised physiological data from multiple anaesthesia devices. Sci Rep. 2018 Jan 24;8(1):1527. [DOI: 10.1038/s41598-018-20062-4](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41598-018-20062-4)

If you are working with VitalDB datasets, the following paper will alleviate the inconvenience of describing in detail the data structure, content, and how to obtain it.

- Lee HC, Park Y, Yoon SB, Yang SM, Park D, Jung CW. VitalDB, a high-fidelity multi-parameter vital signs database in surgical patients. Sci Data. 2022 Jun 8;9(1):279. [DOI: 10.1038/s41597-022-01411-5](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41597-022-01411-5).
