> **Note:** No additional device configuration required.

| Cable | Adapter | Port | VR Device Name |
|-------|---------|------|----------------|
| Custom RJ10 ↔ DB-9F (numeric) | None | P2500 RJ10 port | `Infinity` |
| Analog/Sync cable → MDR14 to RJ45 (waveform) | None | Analog/Sync port | `Infinity` |

**Connection Steps — Numeric Data:**

1. Prepare a cable: **RJ10 pins 3, 2, 4** → **DB-9F pins 2, 3, 5**.2. Connect the **RJ10 end** to the P2500 RJ10 port.3. Connect the **DB-9F end** to the PC via USB-Serial converter.**Connection Steps — Waveform Data:**

1. Obtain an **Analog/Sync cable**.
2. Fabricate an **MDR14 to RJ45 cable** per the pin diagram to connect to the ADC.
