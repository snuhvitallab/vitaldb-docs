## Connection Steps
1. Connect via the Mini-D cable to X5 or X3 of the docking station.> **Note:** Most numeric data recorded every 2 seconds. **Waveform data cannot be extracted** via this interface.

**Waveform Data (Optional):**

Connect to the **X10 Analog/Sync port** (Drager part #4314618).| Pin | Signal |
|-----|--------|
| 12 | CH1 (+) |
| 13 | CH1 (−) |
| 7 | CH2 (+) |
| 6 | CH2 (−) |
