### Drager Infinity Kappa

| Cable | Adapter | Port | VR Device Name |
|-------|---------|------|----------------|
| Mini-D cable (Drager part #5206421) | None | X5 or X3 of docking station | `Infinity` |

**Connection Steps:**

1. Connect via the Mini-D cable to X5 or X3 of the docking station.


   <img src="hardware_images/drager_infinity_kappa_3.png" width="600" alt="Docking station — Mini-D cable connection">

   <img src="hardware_images/drager_infinity_kappa_2.png" width="600" alt="Mini-D cable wiring diagram">

> **Note:** Most numeric data recorded every 2 seconds. **Waveform data cannot be extracted** via this interface.

**Waveform Data (Optional):**

Connect to the **X10 Analog/Sync port** (Drager part #4314618).

<img src="hardware_images/drager_infinity_kappa_1.png" width="600" alt="X10 Analog/Sync port">

| Pin | Signal |
|-----|--------|
| 12 | CH1 (+) |
| 13 | CH1 (−) |
| 7 | CH2 (+) |
| 6 | CH2 (−) |
